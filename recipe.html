Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 2 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup cashew nuts, soaked
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates.
  4. Blend soaked cashew nuts into a smooth paste and add it to the pan.
  5. Stir in red chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
  6. Cut paneer into cubes and add them to the gravy. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Finish by adding cream and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
Chicken Tikka Recipe

Chicken Tikka Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Skewers for grilling

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt to create the marinade.
  2. Add chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring each piece is well coated. Marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight for better flavor.
  3. Preheat the grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread marinated chicken pieces onto skewers.
  5. Grill or bake the chicken until fully cooked, turning occasionally for even cooking.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.
